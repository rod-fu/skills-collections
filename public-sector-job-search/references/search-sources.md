# Search Sources and Ranking Guide

## Official Source Priority

Use sources in this order:

1. National and central government portals:
   - 国家公务员局
   - 中央机关及其直属机构考试录用公务员专题
   - 中国人事考试网
   - 人力资源和社会保障部
   - 国务院国资委 and central SOE recruitment pages

2. Provincial and municipal official portals:
   - 省/市公务员局, 组织部, 人社厅/人社局
   - 省/市人事考试网
   - 事业单位公开招聘专题
   - 地方国资委 and city-owned SOE recruitment pages

3. Employer official pages:
   - 央企集团官网 and subsidiary recruitment pages
   - 国企集团官网, campus recruitment pages, social recruitment pages
   - university, hospital, research institute, school, and public institution personnel pages

4. Discovery-only sources:
   - 高校就业网
   - 智联招聘, 前程无忧, 猎聘, 国聘, 北极星, 应届生求职网
   - WeChat public account reposts

Only shortlist discovery-only results after finding an official notice or official application portal.

## Useful Chinese Query Patterns

Combine profile terms with source terms:

- `site:gov.cn 事业单位 公开招聘 专业 学历 地区`
- `site:*.gov.cn 公务员 招录 职位表 专业 地区`
- `site:sasac.gov.cn 招聘 央企 专业`
- `央企 社会招聘 专业 地区 官方`
- `国企 校园招聘 专业 地区 2026 官方`
- `事业单位 公开招聘 职位表 专业 地区 报名`
- `人才引进 公告 专业 硕士 地区`
- `选调生 招录 公告 专业 学校 地区`
- `人事考试网 事业单位 招聘 地区 专业`

For exact majors, search both the exact major name and the broader discipline category. For China public-sector screening, major wording can be decisive.

## Eligibility Checklist

Check these fields before recommending an opening:

- application deadline has not passed
- age requirement and age calculation date
- education level and degree
- major or discipline category
- graduation year and fresh-graduate status
- work experience years and relevant industry
- professional certificate or qualification
- political status, party membership, grassroots project status
- hukou, birthplace, or residence restrictions
- physical condition, gender, language, driving, shift, or fieldwork requirements
- whether the role is 编制, 聘用制, 劳务派遣, 合同制, 正式员工, or unspecified

## Match Labels

Use:

- `高匹配`: all hard constraints appear satisfied and role direction is strong.
- `中匹配`: hard constraints mostly fit, with moderate uncertainty or weaker role relevance.
- `低匹配`: many soft mismatches, but no obvious hard disqualification.
- `不建议`: clear hard constraint mismatch or deadline passed.
- `需确认`: official notice lacks enough information to decide.

## Ranking Heuristic

Prioritize:

1. Active official notices with near deadlines.
2. Roles where major, degree, and work experience clearly match.
3. Target regions and acceptable organization types.
4. Roles with transparent application process and clear employment nature.
5. Opportunities requiring less extra exam preparation, unless the user prefers exams.

## Final Response Checklist

Every result should include:

- organization
- unit nature
- job title
- location
- deadline
- key requirements
- match label
- reason for match
- risk or unknown
- official link

End with next actions and any missing profile details that would improve search precision.
